PID Platform

This was a project I did in highschool that I petitioned to do as my final assignment for a javascript coding class. The goal of this device was balance a ball on a platform using PID control loops.

Skills:

Onshape CAD, Mechanical Design, C++/Arduino

Outcome:

I used a resistive touchscreen for the platform to recieve the location data of the ball and I used a miniture U-joint with servos and tie rod ends to create the tilting mechanism. I used an Arduino to implement PID control for balancing the ball smoothly.

The project was able to successfully balance the ball on the platform for up to 20 seconds. The materials I chose to use were cost effective but unfortunately they were also poor quality which limited the performance of the device. The materials chosen caused significant backlash within the mechanism which prevented the device from fully stabilizing the ball.

In the future I hope to re-visit this project with a larger budget for more high quality components.
