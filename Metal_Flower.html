Metal Flower

Highschool Art Project: Sculpting a lily flower out of sheet metal.

I modeled this flower after the lilies in my yard. Through this project I learned about how heat-coloring metal works through oxide layers. Although I didn't implement the heat-coloring into the final sculpture.
